About

I am an undergraduate student pursuing dual degrees in Electronic Science and Technology and Administrative Management at Shandong University. My research interests lie in the intersection of Large Language Models (LLMs) and Software Engineering, particularly in code translation, automated program repair, and automated program understanding.
